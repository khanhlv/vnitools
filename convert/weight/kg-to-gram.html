How to convert Kilograms to Grams

1 kilogram (kg) is equal to 1000 grams (g).

1 kg = 1000 g

The mass m in grams (g) is equal to the mass m in kilograms (kg) times 1000:

m(g) = m(kg) × 1000

Example

Convert 5kg to grams:

m(g) = 5 kg × 1000 = 5000 g
