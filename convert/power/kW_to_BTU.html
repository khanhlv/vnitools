How to convert kW to BTU/hr

1 kW = 3412.142 BTU/hr

The power conversion of kW to BTUIT/hr is given by the formula:

P(BTU/hr) = 3412.142 ⋅ P(kW)

 

Example

Convert 5kW to BTU/hr:

P(BTU/hr) = 3412.142 ⋅ 5kW = 17060.71 BTU/hr
