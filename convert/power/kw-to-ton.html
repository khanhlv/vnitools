How to convert kW to tons

One refrigeration ton is equal to 3.5168525 kilowatts:

1 RT = 3.5168525 kW

One kilowatt is equal to 0.284345 refrigeration ton:

1 kW = 0.28434517 RT

 

So the power P in refrigeration tons (RT) is equal to the power P in kilowatts (kW) divided by 3.5168525:

P(RT) = P(kW) / 3.5168525

 

Example

Convert 5 kW to tons:

P(RT) = 5 kW / 3.5168525 = 1.4217 RT
