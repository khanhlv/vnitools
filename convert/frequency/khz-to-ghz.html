How to convert kilohertz to gigahertz

1kHz = 0.000001GHz

or

1GHz = 1000000kHz

Kilohertz to gigahertz formula

The frequency f in hertz (Hz) is equal to the frequency f in kilohertz (kHz) divided by 1000000:

f(GHz) = f(kHz) / 1000000

Example

Convert 3 kilohertz to gigahertz:

f(GHz) = 3kHz / 1000000 = 0.000003GHz
