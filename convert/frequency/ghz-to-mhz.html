How to convert gigahertz to megahertz

1GHz = 1000MHz

or

1MHz = 0.001GHz

Gigahertz to megahertz formula

The frequency f in megahertz (MHz) is equal to the frequency f in gigahertz (GHz) times 1000:

f(MHz) = f(GHz) × 1000

Example

Convert 3 gigahertz to megahertz:

f(MHz) = 3GHz × 1000 = 3000MHz
