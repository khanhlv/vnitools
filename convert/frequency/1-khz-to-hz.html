1 kilohertz to hertz conversion

How to convert 1 kilohertz (kHz) to hertz (Hz).

Calculation

The frequency f in hertz (Hz) is equal to 1 kilohertz (kHz) times 1000:

f(Hz) = 1 kHz × 1000 = 1000 Hz

 

So 1 kilohertz is equal to to 1000 hertz:

1 kHz = 1000 Hz
