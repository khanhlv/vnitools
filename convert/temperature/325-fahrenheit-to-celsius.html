325 Fahrenheit to Celsius conversion

How to convert 325 degrees Fahrenheit to Celsius.

The temperature T in degrees Celsius (°C) is equal to the temperature T in degrees Fahrenheit (°F) minus 32, times 5/9:

T(°C) = (T(°F) - 32) × 5/9 = (325°F - 32) × 5/9 = 162.778°C

So 325 degrees Fahrenheit are equal to 162.778 degrees Celsius:

325°F = 162.778°C
