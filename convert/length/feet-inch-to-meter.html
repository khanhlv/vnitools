How to convert feet+inches to meters

1 foot is equal to 0.3048 meters:

1 ft = 0.3048 m

1 inch is equal to 0.0254 meters:

1 in = 0.0254 m

The distance d in meters (m) is equal to the distance d in feet (ft) times 0.3048 plus the distance d in inches (in) times 0.0254:

d(m) = d(ft) × 0.3048 + d(in) × 0.0254

Example

Convert 2 feet + 3 inches to meters:

d(m) = 2ft × 0.3048 + 3in × 0.0254 = 0.6858m
