How to convert centimeters to yards

1 centimeter is equal to 0.01093613298 yards:

1cm = 100cm/(2.54cm/in)/(36in/yd) = 0.01093613298yd

The distance d in yards (yd) is equal to the distance d in centimeters (cm) divided by 91.44:

d(yd) = d(cm) / 91.44

Example

Convert 20 cm to yards:

d(yd) = 20cm / 91.44 = 0.21872266yd

How many yards in a centimeter

One centimeter is equal to 0.01093613298 yards:

1cm = 1m / 91.44yd/cm = 0.01093613298yd

How many centimeters in a yard

One yard is equal to 91.44 centimeters:

1yd = 91.44×1yd = 91.44cm

How to convert 10cm to yards

Multiply 10 centimeters by 91.44 to get yards:

10cm = 10cm / 91.44cm/yd = 10.9361yd
