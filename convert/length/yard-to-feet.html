How to convert yards to feet

1 yard is equal to 3 feet:

1yd = 3ft

The distance d in feet (ft) is equal to the distance d in yards (yd) times 3:

d(ft) = d(yd) × 3

Example

Convert 20 yards to feet:

d(ft) = 20yd × 3 = 60ft

How many yards in a foot

One foot is equal to 0.33333 yards:

1ft = 1ft / 3 = 0.33333yd

How many feet in a yards

One yard is equal to 3 feet:

1yd = 3×1yd = 3ft

How to convert 10 yards to feet

Multiply 10 yards by 3 to get feet:

10yd = 3×10yd = 30ft
