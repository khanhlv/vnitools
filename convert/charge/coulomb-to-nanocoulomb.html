How to convert coulombs to nanocoulombs

1C = 109nC

or

1nC = 10-9C

Coulombs to nanocoulombs conversion formula

The charge in nanocoulombs Q(nC) is equal to the charge in coulombs Q(C) times 109:

Q(nC) = Q(C) × 109

Example

Convert 3 coulombs to nanocoulombs:

Q(nC) = 3C × 109 = 3⋅109nC
