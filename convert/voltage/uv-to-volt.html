How to convet microvolts to volts

1 V = 106 µV = 1000000 µV

or

1 µV = 10-6 V = 1/1000000 V

Microvolts to volts formula

The voltage V in microvolts (µV) is equal to the voltage V in volts (V) divided by 1000000:

V(V) = V(µV) / 1000000

Example

Convert 3 microvolts to volts:

V(V) = 3µV / 1000000 = 3×10-6 V
