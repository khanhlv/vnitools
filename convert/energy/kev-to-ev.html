How to convert keV to eV

One kiloelectron-volt is equal to 1000 electron-volts:

1keV = 1000eV

The energy in electron-volts E(eV) is equal to the energy in kiloelectron-volts E(keV) times 1000:

E(eV) = E(keV) × 1000

 

Example

Convert 5keV to eV:

E(eV) = 5keV × 1000 = 5000eV
