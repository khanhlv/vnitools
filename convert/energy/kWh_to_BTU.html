How to convert kWh to BTU

1 kWh = 3412.14163312794 BTUIT

 

So the energy in BTUs E(BTU) is equal to 3412.1416.. times the energy in kilowatt-hour E(kWh):

E(BTU) = 3412.14163312794 × E(kWh)

 

Example

Convert 5 kWh to BTU:

E(BTU) = 3412.14163312794 × 5 kWh = 17060.708 BTU
