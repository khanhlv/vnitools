How to convert 2 kJ to joules

How to convert energy of 2 kilojoules (kJ) to joules (J).

The energy E in joules (J) is equal to 2 kilojoules times 1000:

E(J) = 2kJ × 1000 = 2000J

 

So 2 kilojoules (kJ) are equal to 2000 joules (J):

2 kJ = 2000 J
