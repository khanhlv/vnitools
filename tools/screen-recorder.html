Screen Recorder

Your browser does not support screen recording. Open this page with latest version of Chrome, Firefox or Safari desktop browser!

Terms of use: This tool should be used with non-copyrighted content, for personal use only.

How to use the screen recroder

  1. Check/uncheck audio recroding.
  2. Press the record button.
  3. Select the window/browser tab that you want to record in the Share your screen dialog window.
  4. Press the Share button in the Share your screen dialog window.
  5. When recording finished, press the Stop share button.
  6. In the screen recorder page, enter file name.
  7. Press the Save button to save the recording to webm file.
  8. Press the Share button to share the recording with webm file.
